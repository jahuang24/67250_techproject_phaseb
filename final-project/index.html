Welcome to the MonoMuse Museum

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, we invite visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.

We believe art and technology can transform how people connect with ideas, stories, and each other. Our mission is to create accessible and immersive experiences that inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity across all ages. MonoMuse serves students, educators, artists, researchers, families, and lifelong learners in Pittsburgh and beyond. We design exhibitions and programs that welcome first-time museum visitors while offering deeper engagement for returning members and creative professionals.

  • Founding Year: 2018
  • 2019 Milestone: Opened our first interactive digital art gallery.
  • 2022 Milestone: Welcomed our 250,000th visitor and launched youth coding workshops.
  • 2025 Milestone: Expanded to include immersive mixed-reality installations and international artist collaborations.
